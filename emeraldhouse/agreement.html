SCOPE OF WORK

BlaqScale will handle the following for Emerald House Interior Design:

  • Google Ads campaign setup, management, and ongoing optimization targeting high-intent clients seeking interior design, fit out, renovation, furnishing, and pool construction in Dubai
  • Funnel buildout — landing page designed to convert ad traffic into qualified project inquiries and confirmed clients
  • Full tracking setup: every click, every lead, every closed project value recorded and visible to the client
  • Monthly performance reporting with complete transparency — no black box

The goal is to generate qualified high-value project clients and measurable revenue growth for Emerald House.

06
ATTRIBUTION & COMMISSION SCOPE

The 8% commission applies only to projects directly generated and attributable to BlaqScale campaigns. The following are excluded from commission scope:

  • Existing Emerald House clients prior to this agreement
  • Referrals sourced independently by Emerald House
  • Repeat clients not re-acquired through BlaqScale campaigns
  • Any project generated entirely outside of BlaqScale's funnel or ad campaigns

Attribution Window: A lead is considered BlaqScale-attributed for 120 days from the date of first contact through the BlaqScale funnel. Any project that closes within this window is commissionable at 8%.

Multi-channel leads: If a lead first contacts Emerald House through a BlaqScale ad or landing page, they remain BlaqScale-attributed regardless of the channel through which the deal ultimately closes (WhatsApp, phone, in-person).

Dispute process: If attribution of a lead is disputed, both parties will refer to the tracking data in the shared Google Ads dashboard and CRM records as the source of truth. If no resolution is reached within 7 days, both parties agree to review the original lead entry timestamp together and resolve in good faith.
